Zack Dunn

Zack's art take an eerie twist and explores oil painting into a dark imagination. Each piece is filled with detail and gives out a depth of wonder. Even though his style is dark and mysterious, one can still see the beauty in each of his piece in porportions, depth, detail and overall colors.

Behind The Works

Each pieces takes him about 1-3 months to finish completely. After he finalizes a piece, he makes prints out of the design to sell to his platform. He also sells the original piece itself as well. He creates his own dark characters, but at times he also paints iconic horror characters.
