About Zack

 

Zack is from PA. And is now a well known oil painter artist. He always names his pieces with a dark sense of emotion like "Walking with Death" or "Last Dreams". Zack Dunn is a one of the strongest and most promising performers on the contemporary dark art scene. The young and dedicated artist from Pennsylvania agreed to chat with us about him and his mesmerising creations. He served our in the military and after coming back, within 3-4 years he got into the gothic painting scene. Ever since then he grew as an artist.
