Raising Happy Kittens Into Happier Cats

Beth.

As I find homes for these kitties, I like to speak with the adopters about how they should continue to socialize them. It’s so important that kittens don’t develop fearfulness towards situations or people or even other animals. The prime socialization period for kittens is from three to nine weeks, but this work should continue to make them as well-rounded as possible. So don’t think that if they are 10 weeks or even six months, they can’t be socialized anymore. In fact, I have socialized older kittens with great success.
