Ville Valo

Ville is known for his incredible vocals, from sounding like a spoken dark prince to his high angelic pitches. Valo is as well not only just he lead singer for HIM, but plays quite a set of instruments as well.

Valo was first introduced to music through his father, who would often listen to a variety Finnish folk artists. It was during this time that he met fellow bassist and future bandmate Mikko "Mige" Paananen. Valo met guitarist Mikko "Linde" Lindström and the two began playing together in Aurora, with Valo on the drums.

Mikko "Mige" Paananen

Mige is well known for his smooth bass playing notes. From having his intros in the song to adjusting the mood of the song. Mige is a all out talent for the bass.

The Paananen family lived in Oulunkylä, where Mige went to school and met future bandmate Ville Valo. Mige's parents divorced when he was thirteen, and he moved to Tuusula with his mother. While attending the Sibelius Upper Secondary School, Mige moved to Kallio for a year to live with a friend. He then moved to Vallila, where he lived alone for some time, before moving back to live with his mother in Tuusula. Mige began playing drums at age ten, before switching to guitar and eventually bass.
